Welcome to UJ Creative Kits

UJ Creative Kits is a student-focused service that delivers customised academic and creative supply kits directly to UJ students. Whether you’re preparing for exams, working on a design prototype, or completing a last-minute project, we provide the exact tools you need — without the stress of searching for supplies.

Mission

To support UJ students by providing convenient, customised kits that make studying and creative work easier, faster, and more affordable.

Vision

UJ Creative Kits wishes to give access to quality supplies and tools that students need stress free, within budgets, and efficiently.
